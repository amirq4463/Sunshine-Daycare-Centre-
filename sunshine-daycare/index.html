✨ About Us

Nurturing Little Hearts & Minds

Sunshine Daycare Centre is a Private Community-Based Daycare Centre dedicated to providing quality childcare and early education in a loving environment.

🔭

Our Vision

To provide a warm, safe, and joyful environment where every child shines and reaches their full potential.

🎯

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer quality childcare and early education through play-based learning, focusing on the overall development of children.

🏠

Centre Type

We are a Private Community-Based daycare centre, serving families with children ages 1.5 to 6 years in a close-knit community setting.
